Navigator

The navigator class of the ROV competition is designed for intermediate level participants. Tasks are a little harder than the scout class, and require a little more knowledge of electronics and wiring. The Sea Sweepers competed in this class in 2014. The team learned a wealth of knowledge and used it to do so well in the coming years. In our first Navigator competition, we swept the competition, winning first place by a land slide. This caused us to decide to move onto the ranger class.
