    The Sea Sweepers ROV team has been successful in the pool for eight years now. We started out with four boys in fifth grade who were inexperienced, yet interested in robotics. Since then, we have achieved first place overall in the scout, navigator, and ranger classes in the regional M.A.T.E. competition. In 2015, our team won our regional competition and reached international in Newfoundland, Canada. In the 2016 international competition in Houston, the Sea Sweepers placed second overall and earned first place in the product presentation category.
